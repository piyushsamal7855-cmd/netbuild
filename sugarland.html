Frequently Asked Questions

Clear all your doubts before you order a sweet treat.

Do you offer Midnight Delivery in Surat?

Yes! We offer special midnight delivery across Surat. Please place your order at least 24 hours in advance to secure a slot.

Are all your cakes 100% Eggless?

Absolutely! Our entire facility is 100% pure vegetarian and eggless. We use premium substitutes to ensure the cake stays soft and fluffy.

How much time do you need for a Custom Theme Cake?

For custom fondant or multi-tier theme cakes, we request a minimum notice of 2 to 3 days so our chefs can perfect the details.

Can I customize the sugar level?

Yes, health is important! Just mention "Low Sugar" in the extra details box while ordering your custom cake.
